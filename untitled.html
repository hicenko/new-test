  • L0-A Preheat Battery With Air Switch Function

    Preheating battery L0-A is another classical product from Transpring. Based on the fact that some extractions would be thicker or even solidified in cold, even extreme weather, Transpring put forward the concept of preheating and designed preheating battery L0-A.

    To solve the short battery life complaint in the market, L0-A adopted 400/650mAh battery cell with MCU chip, which was more powerful and much safer than most batteries for oil cartridges. We also made change by narrowing the size of the thread connection position, realizing the perfect match with the popular slim cartridge size. Internally, to create a better preheating function and vaping experience, we developed the PCB board ourselves, greatly favored by consumers.

    L0-A has been performing well since its coming out. After the success, we invented more preheating batteries such as MIX2 with manual-automatic function, L10 with bottom knob V-adjusting function. Transpring will insist on innovation and manufacturing as always in the extract vaporizer industry.
